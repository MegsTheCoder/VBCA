Our mission is to bring those who love the outdoors together through casual, non-competitive sporting activities for people of all ages.

The Volkssport Association of British Columbia is a branch of the world-wide Volkssporting Association that is present in 50 countries around the world. If you are among the growing majority who believe that regular outdoor activity in congenial company can improve one's health, fitness and quality of life, then volkssporting is for you. Our goal is to provide unique opportunities are available for safe, healthy exercise without the stresses of speed and strain of endurance. A hallmark of volkssports is that all participants who successfully complete the event of their choice at their own pace, are recognized as "winners"!

VABC has six clubs which primarily host walking events which you are participate on either a Permanent Trail or Scheduled Events. Permanent Trails (PTs) are self-guided walks/rides that are in place over a fixed period of time at a specific location and for Scheduled events we meet at a specified place and start time. Maps and directions are supplied for each participant. The group will start off together and then spread out as participants walk at their own pace. Membership is not required to join us on our walks, but when you choose to join one of our clubs you can earn achievement awards and help to keep this non-profit organization alive. Your first five walks are free and then after that the cost is just $1 to $2 per walk.
